  • Store and Location Mapping: Implemented a feature to link each imported transaction to a specific store and location, providing clarity on the origin of transactions for accurate invoice generation.
  • User Assignment: Assigned each invoice to the corresponding user based on store and location, ensuring that users can access and manage their specific invoicing data.
  • Invoice Validation: Developed validation rules to ensure that all invoice data is accurate, such as matching store locations, transaction details, and invoice amounts.
